Frequently Asked Questions

What is Public Procurement?

Public Procurement is the process by which governments and other publicly – founded entities acquire goods, works and services needed to implement public projects

What are the Procurements Office goals and objectives?

Through our office, the Institute is aiming to improve value for money from the procurement of goods, services and works. It is important to ensure that expenditures are constrained within the Institute’s budgetary provisions and resources are selected and managed in the best possible way. Main objectives like: • Obtaining best value and minimum risk when procuring goods and services and • Ensuring that all staff involved in the procurement process are aware of the relevant law and regulations are vital for Procurement
